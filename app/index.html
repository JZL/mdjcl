If I was shrunk down to the size of a mouse I would go find a squirrel and ride it like in night at the museum. Who wouldn't like to explore the world on the back of a fluffy animal?

Who is Katie Toth?

I'm a junior at Eleanor Roosevelt and this year's parliamentarian. I'll be reviewing the Constitution and seeing if there are any improvements we can make, but I'm mainly looking to institute some simple changes and reforms to the election process so that the platform of potential executive officers can be communicated easier.
